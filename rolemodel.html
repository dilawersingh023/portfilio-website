My Dad



My Dad Inspired Me To Write This. He Has Taken Such A Big Role In My Life.
Whatever I Needed Even If It Was On The Other Side Of The World My Dad Would Go Get It.
I Love Him With All My Heart. He Always Makes Sure I Do My Best In Whatever I Do.
Even Though It Might Be Hard Or Tiring My Dad Makes Me Do It,
I Know It's The Best For Me.
My Dad Is The Perfect Role Model Because He Is Everything A Role Model Should Be,
Loving, Funny, Serious, Nice To Be Around, And Smart.
